About us

Fishing in Norway is a blog about the fishing opportunities in Norway. everything from freshwater fishing in the big forests, to saltwater fishing in the deep North Sea. here you can find a large variety of different information about fishing, like what kinds of fish you find in the waters of Norway, what kinds of lures to use to catch them, and which season they should be fished for.
